\chapter{Entropy in lattice gauge theories}

Lattice gauge theories are the primordial playground for the study of quantum information and topology. 
The lattice $\mathbb{Z}_2$ models have been extensively studied by the quantum computing community,
for its fault tolerance properties and anyonic excitations. To motivate the study of these models,
 we will begin by covering the canonical quantization of yang mills theories in the continuum and 
 then the appropriate lattice discretization.
